`License.txt` sits in its folder. Each kit's `Textures/colormap.png` is our
warm-palette remap of the original (kept as `colormap-original.png`).

## Notes

- **City model:** Kenney Starter Kit: City Builder — the current town look
  (design doc §18 visual-direction update). Only the models + `colormap.png`
  texture are used; we do not use the kit's GridMap builder mechanics.
- **Adventurers is a placeholder** (medieval-fantasy, weapons) pending a modern,
  profession-appropriate, weapon-free character set.
- The KayKit *City Builder Bits* pack was evaluated earlier and dropped in favour
  of the Kenney kit.
